Our Story

Made by People Who Love the Water

Konquest Boats started with a simple idea: a boat should be tough enough to trust and simple enough to enjoy. We build inflatable Zodiacs and pontoon boats the honest way — by hand, one at a time, for people who'd rather be on the water than worrying about their gear.

Whether you're chasing fish at dawn, exploring a hidden cove, or just floating with the family, there's a Konquest built to get you there and back.
